What the GNU Manifesto does is challenge us to think about what kind of culture we want. Do we want one that includes or excludes people?

By excluding people, we risk missing out on potentially valuable cultural contributions. Furthermore, exclusion prevents people from understanding the technology they interface with. This faciliates nefarious people to use code for more manipulative purposes.
