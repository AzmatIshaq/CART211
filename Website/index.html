What is GNU?

"I consider that the Golden Rule requires that if I like a program I must share it with other people who like it." -Richard Stallman

Written by Richard Stallman in 1985, the GNU manifesto remains a thought-provoking document in the world of software development, culture, and business.
The GNU Manifesto proposes the idea of a free software culture where people are free to use, copy, share, and modify software
GNU stands for GNU is Not Unix.
